RoutinePup Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

RoutinePup helps users manage dog routines, reminders, health records, notes, photos, and care information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may access, how that information is used, and the choices available to users.

Information You Provide

RoutinePup may store information you choose to enter or upload in the app, including pet names, breeds, birthdays, weights, routine items, reminders, feeding and medication logs, behavior notes, health records, photos, and documents.

Permissions

RoutinePup may request access to your camera, photos, files, or notifications only when needed for features you choose to use, such as taking a pet photo, uploading a medical document, or receiving reminders. You can manage permissions at any time in your device settings.

How Information Is Used

Information in RoutinePup is used to provide app features, organize pet-care records, support reminders and tracking, and improve app stability and functionality.

Sharing

RoutinePup does not sell your personal data. Information may be disclosed only if you choose to share or export it, or if disclosure is required by law.

Retention and Deletion

Information is kept only as long as needed to provide the app and its features, or until you delete it. If you want help with deletion requests or privacy questions, contact the email below.
